--- page-title: Our Process ---

Stages

  • Discovery
  • Design
  • Development
  • Debug
  • Deploy

Discovery Stage

The discovery stage is the most important. During this stage, we plan to get to know everything we can about the project and form a clear project definition. This definition consists of a project timeframe, summary, goals, target audiences, and the intended message. A paper sketch of any intended technologies or layouts that the client would like to see in their site is noted.

Design Stage

Taking in all of the project notes and paper sketching and technologies, a sitemap is created. From there the pages are put into a visual representation and the site architecture is created. The designer will then bring together the ideas and create a usable site in a wireframe layout. From there we will work closely with the client to create the overall look and user experience of the site.

After the website has been designed, we move on to the development stage.
